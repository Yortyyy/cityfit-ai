# Quality of Life Index Notes

The Numbeo Quality of Life Index is used as a baseline benchmark in CityFit AI.

It provides a general city-level quality-of-life comparison based on several underlying dimensions.

## Metrics Used by CityFit

CityFit uses the following city-level metrics:

- Quality of Life Index
- Purchasing Power Index
- Safety Index
- Healthcare Index
- Cost of Living Index
- Property Price to Income Ratio
- Traffic Commute Index
- Pollution Index
- Climate Index

## Why CityFit Adds Personalization

A general quality-of-life score may not fit every person equally.

For example:

- A remote worker may not care much about commute time.
- A renter may care more about rent and cost of living than property purchase affordability.
- A family may prioritize safety, healthcare, and pollution.
- A high-income user may accept higher cost for better quality of life.
- A climate-sensitive user may prioritize climate and pollution over purchasing power.

CityFit uses the baseline quality-of-life ranking as an input, then adjusts recommendations using user-defined priorities.

## Interpretation

A high Quality of Life Index means the city performs well overall under the baseline methodology.

A high CityFit Score means the city performs well for the selected user preferences.

These two rankings may differ.
